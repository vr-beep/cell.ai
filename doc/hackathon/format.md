## Two Phases

### Phase 1: Research (12PM-2PM, Saturday)
- 1-hour tutorial with Monomer staff on seeding density experiment
- 1 hour to answer key research questions before building
- Deliverables: select 3 components, define ranges, precipitation rules, DOE vs Bayesian decision

### Phase 2: Build (2PM Saturday - 3PM Sunday)
- ~24 hours of experimental execution (robots sleep at midnight)
- Build autonomous workflow, run experiments, iterate
- Goal: **BUILD THE BEST MEDIA**
